Different Technological courses

Web-Development

Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites.It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites.

Mobile-App development

Mobile application development is the process of creating software applications that run on a mobile device

Big-Data Analytics

Big data analytics is the often complex process of examining big data to uncover informationthat can help organizations make informed business decisions.

Cybersecurity

Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks.

Blockchain Technology

Blockchain is a shared, immutable ledger that facilitates the process of recording transactions and tracking assets in a business network.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.
